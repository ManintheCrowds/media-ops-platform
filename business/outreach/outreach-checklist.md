# Weekly Outreach Checklist

A systematic checklist for consistent client outreach and business development.

## Weekly Goals

- **Target:** Reach out to 10-20 potential clients per week
- **Follow-ups:** Follow up with 5-10 previous contacts
- **Content:** Post 3-5 times on LinkedIn
- **Networking:** Connect with 10-15 new people on LinkedIn

---

## Monday: Planning & Research

### Morning Tasks

- [ ] Review previous week's outreach results
- [ ] Identify 10-20 new prospects for the week
- [ ] Research each prospect (company, role, needs)
- [ ] Prepare personalized outreach messages
- [ ] Update CRM with new prospects

### Afternoon Tasks

- [ ] Draft email templates for the week
- [ ] Prepare LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Schedule social media content for the week
- [ ] Review and update outreach templates

### End of Day

- [ ] Set goals for the week
- [ ] Prepare Tuesday's outreach materials

---

## Tuesday: Initial Outreach

### Morning Tasks

- [ ] Send 5-10 personalized connection requests on LinkedIn
- [ ] Send 5-10 cold outreach emails
- [ ] Follow up on proposals sent last week
- [ ] Respond to any inquiries from previous outreach

### Afternoon Tasks

- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (technical content or insight)
- [ ] Engage with others' LinkedIn posts (5-10 comments)
- [ ] Share relevant content from your network
- [ ] Update CRM with outreach activities

### End of Day

- [ ] Review responses and inquiries
- [ ] Schedule follow-ups for non-responders
- [ ] Prepare Wednesday's outreach

---

## Wednesday: Follow-Ups & Engagement

### Morning Tasks

- [ ] Follow up on emails sent Monday/Tuesday (if no response)
- [ ] Follow up on LinkedIn messages
- [ ] Respond to any new inquiries
- [ ] Check proposal statuses

### Afternoon Tasks

- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (case study or value content)
- [ ] Engage with industry discussions
- [ ] Share case study or project highlight
- [ ] Connect with people who engaged with your content

### End of Day

- [ ] Review engagement metrics
- [ ] Update CRM with interactions
- [ ] Prepare Thursday's outreach

---

## Thursday: Content & Thought Leadership

### Morning Tasks

- [ ] Send 5-10 new outreach emails
- [ ] Follow up on pending proposals
- [ ] Respond to inquiries
- [ ] Schedule client calls (if any)

### Afternoon Tasks

- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (industry insight or thought leadership)
- [ ] Write blog post or article (if time permits)
- [ ] Engage with community discussions
- [ ] Share educational content

### End of Day

- [ ] Review week's progress
- [ ] Identify what's working
- [ ] Adjust strategy if needed
- [ ] Prepare Friday's tasks

---

## Friday: Review & Relationship Building

### Morning Tasks

- [ ] Follow up on all outstanding communications
- [ ] Send final follow-ups for the week
- [ ] Respond to all inquiries
- [ ] Schedule follow-ups for next week

### Afternoon Tasks

- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (community engagement or question)
- [ ] Engage with your network
- [ ] Share weekend reading or resources
- [ ] Thank people who engaged with your content

### End of Day

- [ ] Review week's metrics:
  - [ ] Outreach sent
  - [ ] Responses received
  - [ ] Meetings scheduled
  - [ ] Proposals sent
  - [ ] Content engagement
- [ ] Update CRM with all activities
- [ ] Plan next week's focus areas
- [ ] Celebrate wins!

## Metrics to Track

### Outreach Metrics

- **Outreach Sent:** Total emails/LinkedIn messages sent
- **Response Rate:** % of outreach that gets responses
- **Meeting Rate:** % of responses that lead to meetings
- **Proposal Rate:** % of meetings that lead to proposals
- **Close Rate:** % of proposals that convert to projects

### Content Metrics

- **Posts Published:** Number of social media posts
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, comments, shares
- **Reach:** Number of people who saw your content
- **Click-Through Rate:** Clicks on links in posts
- **Follower Growth:** New connections/followers

### Business Metrics

- **New Clients:** Number of new clients acquired
- **Revenue:** Total revenue from outreach efforts
- **Pipeline Value:** Total value of opportunities in pipeline
- **Average Deal Size:** Average project value
- **Sales Cycle:** Time from first contact to close

## Tools & Systems

### CRM System

- Track all prospects and contacts
- Log all outreach activities
- Set follow-up reminders
- Generate reports
- **Options:** HubSpot (free), Pipedrive, Salesforce

### Email Tracking

- Track email opens and clicks
- Set follow-up reminders
- **Options:** Yesware, Mixmax, Mailtrack

### Social Media Management

- Schedule posts
- Track engagement
- Manage multiple platforms
- **Options:** Buffer, Hootsuite, LinkedIn Scheduler

### Calendar Management

- Schedule meetings
- Set reminders
- Block time for outreach
- **Options:** Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly

## Best Practices

### Consistency

- **Daily:** Check and respond to communications
- **Weekly:** Send consistent outreach
- **Monthly:** Review and adjust strategy

### Personalization

- Always personalize outreach
- Research each prospect
- Reference specific details
- Show genuine interest

### Value First

- Provide value in every interaction
- Share helpful resources
- Offer insights and expertise
- Build relationships, not just sell

### Follow-Up

- Follow up systematically
- Don't give up after one attempt
- Space follow-ups appropriately
- Know when to move on

### Track Everything

- Log all activities in CRM
- Track what works and what doesn't
- Measure results
- Adjust based on data

---

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- ❌ Inconsistent outreach
- ❌ Generic, non-personalized messages
- ❌ Not following up
- ❌ Only focusing on new prospects
- ❌ Ignoring existing network
- ❌ Not tracking metrics
- ❌ Giving up too early
- ❌ Being too salesy
- ❌ Not providing value
- ❌ Neglecting content creation

## Weekly Review Questions

At the end of each week, ask yourself:

1. **What worked well this week?**
2. **What didn't work?**
3. **What should I do differently next week?**
4. **What opportunities am I missing?**
5. **How can I improve my outreach?**
6. **What content resonated with my audience?**
7. **What relationships should I nurture?**
8. **What goals did I achieve?**
9. **What goals need more focus?**
10. **What's my plan for next week?**

## Success Indicators

You're on the right track if:

- ✅ Consistent outreach activity
- ✅ Steady response rate (10-20%)
- ✅ Regular meetings scheduled
- ✅ Proposals being sent
- ✅ Projects being closed
- ✅ Growing network
- ✅ Increasing engagement
- ✅ Building reputation
- ✅ Generating referrals
- ✅ Steady pipeline

**Remember:** Outreach is a marathon, not a sprint. Consistency and providing value are key to long-term success. Focus on building relationships, and the business will follow.
