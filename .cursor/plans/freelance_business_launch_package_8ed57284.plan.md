- Problem: Need for unified self-hosted service management

- Technologies: FastAPI, Docker, PostgreSQL, Nginx, Prometheus, Grafana
- Results: Unified SSO, centralized monitoring, automated health checks
- Location: `portfolio/case-studies/self-hosted-platform.html`

**Case Study 2: Archivist at Suburban Cable Commission**

- Problem: Manual, error-prone government meeting transcription
- Solution: Automated transcription and captioning pipeline
- Technologies: WhisperX, Flask, FastAPI, Celery, PostgreSQL, Prometheus
- Results: 256+ caption files, 93.5% success rate, 100% uptime, 9 cities supported
- Location: `portfolio/case-studies/archivist.html`

**Format:**

## Implementation Approach

1. **Portfolio Website**: Modern, clean design with focus on case studies and services
2. **Case Studies**: Data-driven narratives highlighting technical depth and business impact
3. **Service Packages**: Clear, scoped offerings with transparent pricing
4. **Business Templates**: Professional, legally-sound (with disclaimers) templates

## Design Principles

- **Professional yet approachable** - Technical credibility without being intimidating
- **Data-driven** - Emphasize metrics and concrete results
- **Clear value proposition** - AI-powered speed + infrastructure expertise
- **Mobile-first** - Responsive design for all devices
- **Fast loading** - Optimized assets and minimal dependencies
- **SEO-friendly** - Semantic HTML, proper headings, meta tags
