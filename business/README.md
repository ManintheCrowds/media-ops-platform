# Business Infrastructure Documentation

Complete business setup and operational documentation for freelance development business.

## Structure

### Templates (`templates/`)

- `proposal-template.md` - Standard project proposal template
- `contract-template.md` - Service agreement template
- `statement-of-work-template.md` - Detailed SOW template

**Note:** All templates should be reviewed by an attorney before use.

### Documentation (`docs/`)

- `project-management-workflow.md` - Complete project management workflow adapted from AI principles

### Setup Guides (`setup/`)

- `llc-setup-guide.md` - Step-by-step LLC formation guide
- `contracts-checklist.md` - Essential contracts and legal documents checklist
- `invoicing-setup.md` - Invoicing system setup and recommendations
- `accounting-setup.md` - Accounting software setup and best practices
- `tax-considerations.md` - Tax planning and considerations for freelancers

### Outreach Materials (`outreach/`)

- `email-templates.md` - Professional email templates for client outreach
- `linkedin-templates.md` - LinkedIn connection and message templates
- `proposal-follow-up.md` - Proposal follow-up sequence and templates
- `social-media-content.md` - Social media content ideas and templates
- `outreach-checklist.md` - Weekly outreach checklist and system

## Platform Context

This business documentation supports the self-hosted platform by providing:

## Usage

### Getting Started

1. **Review Templates:** Customize templates for your business
2. **Consult Professionals:** Review contracts with attorney, accounting with CPA
3. **Set Up Systems:** Follow setup guides for invoicing, accounting, etc.
4. **Start Outreach:** Use outreach materials to begin client acquisition
5. **Document Services:** Use templates to document platform services delivered

### Customization

- Update business name (currently "Xanadu Media")
- Customize templates with your specific terms
- Adjust pricing in templates
- Modify workflows to fit your process
- Add your own examples and case studies

## Important Disclaimers

- **Tax Information:** Consult with a CPA or tax professional for tax advice
- **Business Formation:** Laws vary by state/jurisdiction
- **Compliance:** Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal regulations
