# Email Templates for Client Outreach

Professional email templates for reaching out to potential clients.

## Cold Outreach Email Template

### Template 1: Initial Introduction

**Subject:** Quick question about [Company Name]'s [specific need/area]

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [Company Name] is [specific observation about their business/website/product]. I specialize in [your service] and thought you might be interested in how [specific benefit/value proposition].

I recently helped [similar company/industry] [specific result/metric]. For example:
- [Result 1]
- [Result 2]

Would you be open to a brief 15-minute call to discuss how [your service] could help [Company Name] [specific goal]?

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
Xanadu Media  
[Email] | [Phone] | [Website]

## Best Practices

### Subject Lines

- Keep it short (under 50 characters)
- Be specific and relevant
- Create curiosity or value
- Personalize when possible
- Avoid spam words

**Good Examples:**
- "Quick question about [Company]'s API integration"
- "[Mutual Contact] suggested I reach out"
- "Solving [Problem] for [Industry] companies"

**Bad Examples:**
- "Amazing opportunity!!!"
- "You won't believe this!"
- "Free consultation"

### Email Body

- **Personalize:** Always personalize, never send generic emails
- **Be Brief:** Get to the point quickly
- **Add Value:** Offer something valuable (insight, resource, case study)
- **Clear CTA:** Have a clear call-to-action
- **Professional:** Maintain professional tone

### Timing

- **Best Days:** Tuesday-Thursday
- **Best Times:** 9-10 AM, 2-3 PM (recipient's timezone)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons
- **Follow-ups:** Space 1 week, 2 weeks, 1 month apart

### Personalization Tips

- Research the company and person
- Reference specific details about their business
- Mention mutual connections
- Reference recent news or achievements
- Show you understand their industry

## Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Act (US)

- Include physical mailing address
- Provide clear opt-out mechanism
- Honor opt-out requests promptly
- Don't use deceptive subject lines
- Identify message as advertisement (if applicable)

### GDPR (EU)

- Get explicit consent
- Provide privacy notice
- Allow data access and deletion
- Honor opt-out requests
- Document consent

---

**Remember:** Always personalize emails, provide value, and respect the recipient's time. Quality over quantity!
